Design notes

The score is deterministic, not probabilistic. That is intentional: deterministic scores are auditable, explainable to agents, and tunable without retraining. Each weight was calibrated against a 30-ticket enterprise support sample and should be reviewed weekly against false-positive and false-negative routing outcomes. AI triage is a Month 3 upgrade — once the taxonomy, KB, and routing lanes are stable enough to classify into.
